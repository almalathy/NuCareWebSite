About Us

We are a start up in Penang, Malaysia. The first Pharmacy in the bustling new township in Batu Kawan, Bandar Cassia.

We aim to provide our customers with a fresh New (Nu) approach to pharmacy needs.

We pride ourselves on personal patient care both medically and holistically.

NuCare Sdn Bhd is an investment holding company, which engages in the operation of community pharmacy including retailing of pharmaceutical, healthcare, and personal care products. The company's product offerings include scheduled drugs, over-the-counter drugs, health supplements, personal care products, healthcare devices, health food, confectionery, beverages, and household products.


WE CARE AT NUCARE

Vision

Reaching people and touching lives as a leading provider of valued medicines and services.

Mission

We are passionately committed to providing diverse pharmacy and other health care products and services, emphasizing on service excellence, innovation, and cost-effectiveness for the benefit of our patients. To be an advocate of healthy lifestyles to the communities we serve. To create an amazing work environment and career advancements for our dedicated team members.

Our Values

  • Quality - Get it right the first time.
  • Reliability - Maintain efficiency & discipline in all processes & systems and fulfil the promises made to stakeholders & customers.
  • Trust – Always transparent in dealings with people.
  • Consistency- Endeavour to bring new products to the market & consistently deliver value to stakeholders.
  • Innovation - Implement new ideas & technologies to meet unmet needs and move ahead of times.

Why need to work with us?

  • We care for the well being of others not only by selling medical products but by also giving away a portion of our profits.
  • Always put ‘we’ before ‘me’.
  • Advocate social enterprising for the less fortunate around us.
  • Transparency in all transaction including accounting & money flows.
