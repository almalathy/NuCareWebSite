About Us

We are a start up in Penang, Malaysia. The first Pharmacy in the bustling new township in Batu Kawan, Bandar Cassia. We aim to provide our customers with a fresh New (Nu) approach to pharmacy needs. We pride ourselves on personal patient care both medically and holistically. NuCare Sdn Bhd is an investment holding company, which engages in the operation of community pharmacy including retailing of pharmaceutical, healthcare, and personal care products. The company's product offerings include scheduled drugs, over-the-counter drugs, health supplements, personal care products, healthcare devices, healthy food, confectionery, beverages, and household products.


Vision

Reaching people and touching lives as a leading provider of valued medicines and services.

Mission
